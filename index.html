About Me

I am a full stack web developer. I started as a COBOL mainframe developer with DST and was given the opportunity to join the front-end web development team. I was responsible for providing front-end support and maintaining the websites for leading mutual fund companies. I was self taught but realized I needed to quickly expand my skill set to work as a web developer full time. I joined the KU Coding Bootcamp to learn full stack development and enjoyed the challenge. I learned a lot of new skills and would love the opportunity to apply those skills to a new job. I look forward to continuing to learn and expand my skills throughout my career.


Technical Skills:

HTML5, CSS, SAAS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Handlebars, AJAX, RESTful APIs, Express, React, Node, MongoDB, MySQL, Firebase, Heroku, Git, Jira, COBOL, Easytrieve, JCL, SQL, DB2, VSAM, Datcom, File Manager, Access Database, NDM, and FTP.
